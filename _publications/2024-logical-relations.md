The "sibling" of the FoSSaCS 2024 paper. We switch our attention to logical relations and contextual equivalence. However, rather than simply tweaking a few definitions, we extend our theory to accommodate step-indexed logical relations for iso-recursive types.

This is probably my favorite HO GSOS paper to date. It might not be groundbreaking, as the key breakthrough had already happened a few months earlier (i.e. in the FoSSaCS 2024 paper), but it is very nicely written, it unifies concepts such as applicative bisimilarity and logical equivalence under the same coalgebraic roof and it basically gives sound (w.r.t. contextual equivalence) logical relations "for free"... as long as one works within the confines of the higher-order abstract GSOS framework.

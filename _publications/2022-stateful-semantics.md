My first paper with the Erlangen group, and a significant leap in terms of writing style and technical quality. This paper proposes a new abstract format for imperative, first-order languages, which I stumbled onto by accident. Initially, we thought we could smoothly extend the original theory of Abstract GSOS into this, very similar, abstract format. As it turns out, this is not quite possible, and the end result is a more syntactic theory than originally intended.

And thus, this attempt of reconciling Abstract GSOS with imperative languages, although a nice result in its own right, fell short once again. The first important attempt was done by Faris Abou-Saleh and Dirk Pattinson but, like ours, they departed too much from the original framework for their results to be considered a "smooth" extension.

The entire story, however, is yet to be written :).

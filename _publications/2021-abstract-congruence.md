This work marked the first time I developed any kind of meaningful theory of program equivalence at a sufficiently abstract level. I did my damnest to reduce congruence of first-order weak bisimilarity to something simple, in this case three abstract, yet (more-or-less) syntactic, conditions on the operational semantics of the language in question. I am quite happy with the end result.

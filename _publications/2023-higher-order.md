This works gives an answer to the long-standing issue of applying Turi and Plotkin's Abstract GSOS framework, or at least its underlying ideas, to higher-order languages. It also marked the beginning of the HO (Abstract) GSOS programme. There is really not much more to say; I will simply add that nailing this result was an arduous task, and it took us a while to figure out how to model the lambda calculus. I really hope you enjoy :).

PS: The title is a homage to the original Turi and Plotkin paper from 1997.
